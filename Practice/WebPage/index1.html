Lets Talk . . .

When I sit and do nothing then proabably I do the best thing. I think about myself.
I think about you. I think about you and I think about the world. After seeing this
world so much I realised one thing that most of the people are devoid of one basic
thing and that is they aren't humble.

I travelled a lot and I found that the world is really in grief. There are people in the
world who have nothing to eat and striving for happiness. I talked to few and you
know what, what actually surprised me? Though they have nothing with themselves
but still they managed to greet me with a smile.

A smile and humbless can change a lot in this cruel world. Though a smile can't
provide them food to eat, neither it can provide them those necessary things for
the survival but a smile can really change their view towards this world. A smile
can make them smile. Our humbleness can make the other person humble too.

To make this world better

Lets take a pledge

To bring a smile on the face of at least a single person.
